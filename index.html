About me

A counselor who's been inside the system

I'm Wonny — a Certified Rehabilitation Counselor (CRC) based in Seoul, with a background that spans Korean government welfare institutions, U.S. clinical training, and front-line international student support.

I spent four years working inside Korea's Workers' Compensation and Welfare system, then earned my M.S. in Rehabilitation Counseling from the University of Wisconsin-Stout, where I worked as a job coordinator helping individuals with disabilities enter the workforce.

Back in Korea, I now work as a consultant for international students — and I'm building toward a Korean Counseling Psychology license through 한국상담심리학회. I also speak Vietnamese, which lets me serve one of Korea's largest immigrant communities directly.

My approach is evidence-based but deeply human. I don't just help you write a better resume — I help you understand what kind of work fits who you actually are.
